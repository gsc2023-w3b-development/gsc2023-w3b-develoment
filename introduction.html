What Is Pokémon

Pokémon, derived from the term Pocket Monsters, stands as a cultural phenomenon that originated in Japan, capturing hearts and imaginations worldwide. Conceived by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori, the franchise was introduced by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures in 1996, forever changing the landscape of entertainment.
